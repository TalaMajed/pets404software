how pets404 works?

How do I report a found pet?

If you've found a pet, click on the "Report Found Pets" button. Provide details about the pet, such as Type , Breed , and the location where you found it. Upload a photo if possible. This information helps the rightful owner recognize their pet.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it securely.

How do I report a lost pet?

To report a lost pet, click on the "Add Lost Pet" button on our homepage. Fill in the required details such as the pet's name, breed, color, last seen location, and upload a recent photo. Providing detailed information increases the chances of finding your pet.

Is my personal information safe on this website?

Yes, we use encryption and other security measures to protect your personal information and keep it confidential.

account managemnet

creating an account:

To create an account, please fill out the following fields:

a. Username:
Choose a unique username that you will use to log in. This could be your name or a fun nickname.
Example: PetLover123

b. Email:
Enter a valid email address. This will be used for account verification and communication.
Make sure it's an email you check regularly.
Example: youremail@example.com

c. Password:
Create a strong password to keep your account secure.
Your password should be at least 8 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and special characters.
Example: P@ssw0rd!23

logging in

To log into your account, please follow these steps:

1. Username or Email:
Enter the username or email address associated with your account.
Example: PetLover123 or youremail@example.com

2. Password:
Enter your password.
Remember, your password is case-sensitive, so be sure to enter it exactly as you set it.

3. Submit:
Click the "Login" button to access your account.
